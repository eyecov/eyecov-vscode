# Code of Conduct

Be useful. Be direct. Do not be a jerk.

## Expected

- Keep discussion about the work.
- Give concrete bug reports and review comments.
- Assume people are trying to improve the project until they prove otherwise.
- Respect boundaries around private code, security reports, and personal data.

## Not Accepted

- Harassment or threats.
- Personal attacks.
- Publishing private information.
- Derailing issues or pull requests into unrelated fights.
- Ignoring maintainer decisions after a clear answer.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block people who make the project harder to maintain.
